Squidstribution Game Jam

For this project my team was given a randomly generated X-statement that we had to develop into a game. Our X-statement was "A god game, where you distribute squids to gain karma". We took this to be a game about a kaiju squid monster destroying a city.

We put a focus on environment destruction and took inspiration from the Chinese belief of Feng Shui with more ornate and colourful buildings giving negative karma when destroyed and more utilitarian, grey concrete buildings giving negative karma.

In this project I took on a greater managment role, leading several scrum meetings and supervising weekly merges. I also took on the task of creating cinematic camera angles and whilst not all of them made it into the final game, I am very pleased with the ones that did.

Unfortunatly I am unable to provide a build of this project.
